a biopic = биографический фильм = Things that happen in the life of a real person
a romantic comedy = романтическая комедия = Funny things happen. Two people fall in love
a fantasy film = фэнтези = Strange things happen in an imaginary world
a psychological thriller = психологический триллер = The main character has mental problems
a mystery = детективная загадка / мистика = Somebody gets murdered and a detective tries to find out who did it, but it all seems really strange
a docudrama = докудрама = A documentary made more interesting with some parts acted
a disaster movie = фильм-катастрофа = Terrible things happen, but people survive
a period drama / a costume drama = историческая драма / костюмированная драма = People dressed up in old-fashioned costumes
a science fiction film (sci-fi) = научная фантастика = A story about the science of the future
an action/adventure film = боевик / приключенческий фильм = Heroes chase and fight each other
a crime film = криминальный фильм = The good guy (the detective) finds the bad guy (the criminal)
