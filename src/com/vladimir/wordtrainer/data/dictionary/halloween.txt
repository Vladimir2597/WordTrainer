treat = угощение = noun. Something nice to eat, often sweets or candy, etc.
trick = шутка, розыгрыш = noun. A joke or prank.
bobbing for apples = ловля яблок = phrase. A traditional Halloween game: apples are put in a barrel of water and people try to remove them using only their mouths.
to roam = бродить, скитаться = verb. To walk or travel with no clear purpose or direction.
Samhain = Самайн = noun. A holiday, falling on the night of 31 October to 1 November, celebrated by the ancient Celts as the beginning of winter and the new year, and a time during which the spirits of the dead could return to the earth.
souling = моление за умерших = noun. The Halloween tradition of praying for people's dead relatives.
guising = переодевание = noun. A Celtic tradition when young men with masked faces, dressed in white, impersonated the dead.
hijinks = шалости, проделки = noun. Tricky or waggish behavior.
