a biopic = биографический фильм = noun. Things that happen in the life of a real person
a romantic comedy = романтическая комедия = noun. Funny things happen. Two people fall in love
a fantasy film = фэнтези = noun. Strange things happen in an imaginary world
a psychological thriller = психологический триллер = noun. The main character has mental problems
a mystery = детективная загадка / мистика = noun. Somebody gets murdered and a detective tries to find out who did it, but it all seems really strange
a docudrama = докудрама = noun. A documentary made more interesting with some parts acted
a disaster movie = фильм-катастрофа = noun. Terrible things happen, but people survive
a period drama / a costume drama = историческая драма / костюмированная драма = noun. People dressed up in old-fashioned costumes
a science fiction film / sci-fi = научная фантастика = noun. A story about the science of the future
an action/adventure film = боевик / приключенческий фильм = noun. Heroes chase and fight each other
a crime film = криминальный фильм = noun. The good guy (the detective) finds the bad guy (the criminal)
