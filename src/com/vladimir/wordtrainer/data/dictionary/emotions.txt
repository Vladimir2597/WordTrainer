envious = завистливый = adj. feeling unhappy because someone else has something you want
confused = растерянный / сбитый с толку = adj. unable to understand something clearly or to think clearly
anxious = тревожный = adj. feeling worried or nervous about something that might happen
disappointed = разочарованный = adj. feeling sad because something was not as good as you hoped or expected
cross = сердитый / раздражённый = adj. slightly angry or annoyed
upset/sad = расстроенный = adj. feeling unhappy, worried, or emotionally hurt
excited/delighted = взволнованный / воодушевлённый = adj. feeling very happy and eager about something
bored = скучающий = adj. not interested because nothing is happening or something is not enjoyable
frightened/shocked/terrified = напуганный = adj. feeling fear; scared
embarrassed/ashamed = смущённый = adj. feeling uncomfortable because you think you look silly or made a mistake
proud = гордый = adj. feeling pleased and satisfied about your achievements or someone you care about
relieved = облегченный = adj. feeling happy because something unpleasant did not happen or has ended
suspicious = подозрительный = adj. feeling that something is wrong or that someone is not honest
