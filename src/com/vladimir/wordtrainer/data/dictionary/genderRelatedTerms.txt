patriarchy = патриархат = noun. A society or system in which men hold the primary power and leadership positions
feminine = женственный / женский = adj. Having qualities or characteristics traditionally associated with women
masculine = мужественный / мужской = adj. Having qualities or characteristics traditionally associated with men
gender = гендер = noun. The physical and/or social condition of being male or female
misogyny = мизогиния / женоненавистничество = noun. Dislike of or prejudice against women
misandry = мизандрия / мужененавистничество = noun. Dislike of or prejudice against men
sexism = сексизм = noun. Prejudice or discrimination based on a person's sex
identity = идентичность = noun. The distinguishing character, personality, or sense of self of an individual
