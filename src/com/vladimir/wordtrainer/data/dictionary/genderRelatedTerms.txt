patriarchy = патриархат = A society or system in which men hold the primary power and leadership positions
feminine = женственный / женский = Having qualities or characteristics traditionally associated with women
masculine = мужественный / мужской = Having qualities or characteristics traditionally associated with men
gender = гендер = The physical and/or social condition of being male or female
misogyny = мизогиния / женоненавистничество = Dislike of or prejudice against women
misandry = мизандрия / мужененавистничество = Dislike of or prejudice against men
sexism = сексизм = Prejudice or discrimination based on a person’s sex
identity = идентичность = The distinguishing character, personality, or sense of self of an individual
